MCC & sMCC — Flagship Cellulose Excipients for Modern Solid Dosage Forms

Grecon's MCC portfolio lets you match flow, density and compression exactly to your press or encapsulator. Our sMCC line adds silica-driven glide and hardness—perfect for high-speed, low-API or moisture-sensitive formulations.
